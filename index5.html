UniChat

Questions about Uni? Our 150 mentors have the answers.

If you’re a Sixth Form student applying to Uni, it’s likely you have a few questions! What will my halls be like? How will my course be structured week to week? What’s the Sports Centre like?

Wouldn’t it be great if you could have a direct line to a student already studying a similar course, or already enrolled at your dream Uni?

Now you can!

UniChat pairs you with a current student who is enrolled in a similar course, or at a similar Uni, to the one you’re interested in, depending on your preferences. Using our webchat, you can ask your new mentor all the questions you have about your next big step. It’s as easy as that!
